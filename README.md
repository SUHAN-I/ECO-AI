## рҹҡҖ Executive Summary

**ECO AI** is a Multimodal Generative AI platform purpose-built to digitize Pakistan's informal waste sector вҖ” and scale globally. By fusing **Llama 4 Scout Vision** for instant waste identification with **Llama 3.3-70B** for deep economic reasoning over a **FAISS-powered RAG pipeline**, ECO AI transforms discarded materials into traceable, profitable resources.

| What | Impact |
|------|--------|
| рҹ“ё Photo вҶ’ instant waste classification | Any smartphone, any condition |
| рҹ’° Real-time Misri Shah scrap pricing | Collectors bypass middlemen entirely |
| рҹҸӯ Urdu-first manufacturing SOPs | Waste вҶ’ industrial-grade products |
| рҹҡҖ AI-generated micro-startup plans | Collectors become entrepreneurs |
| рҹӣӮ Digital Product Passport | Corporate EPR compliance, 2026-ready |
| рҹҺӨ Whisper voice interface | Solves literacy barrier at grassroots level |

Pakistan generates **30+ million metric tons** of municipal solid waste annually вҖ” yet only a fraction is formally recycled. ECO AI closes this gap by delivering industrial-grade intelligence directly to grassroots collectors, turning waste into wealth at scale.
